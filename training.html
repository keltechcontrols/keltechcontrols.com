Group Training & Workshops

Team-level development built around real turnaround scheduling.

Workshops are delivered virtually and tailored to the group's current capability — whether that's a planning team building P6 foundations or a coordinator group that wants stronger turnaround scheduling judgment.

All sessions are experience-based and owner-side focused. The goal is practical improvement in how your team reads, contributes to, and challenges a P6 schedule — not generic software training.

Workshop Formats

Four areas of focus, built around your team's needs.

P6 Fundamentals for Teams Activity setup, logic development, float interpretation, and common mistakes. Useful for planning teams who work around P6 but want to build real confidence reading and contributing to schedules.
Turnaround Scheduling Concepts Shutdown and startup sequencing, inspection integration, craft loading, and how a well-built schedule supports execution decisions. Practical and owner-side focused — covers what courses miss.
Schedule Review & Readiness Teaches teams how to read and challenge a schedule — identifying logic gaps, float risk, and sequencing issues before they surface in the field. Well-suited for coordinator groups and planners moving toward scheduling roles.
